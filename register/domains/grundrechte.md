# Grundrechte & Verfassung — fundamental rights and the constitution

Governing law: **BV (SR 101)** · file `cc-1999-404.md` · Stand 2024-03-03 · coverage ✓ (Grundrechte Art. 7–36 complete)
Scope: the Bundesverfassung. Fundamental rights bind state action; their effect between private parties is
indirect (Art. 35). Restrictions need the conditions of Art. 36.
