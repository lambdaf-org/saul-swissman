# Strafrecht (Allgemeiner Teil) — general principles of criminal law

Governing law: **StGB (SR 311.0)** · file `cc-54-757_781_799.md` · Stand 2026-01-01 · coverage **AT only (Art. 1–64)**
Scope: the general part of the Criminal Code — principles, justifications, sentencing, measures.

> **Corpus gap — read before any "is X a crime?" question.** This copy contains the Allgemeiner Teil only
> (Art. 1–64, with internal gaps 37–41 and 53). The **Besonderer Teil is absent**: the actual offences —
> Tötung (Art. 111 ff.), Körperverletzung (122 ff.), Diebstahl (Art. 139), Betrug (Art. 146), Nötigung
> (181), Ehrverletzung (173 ff.) — are **not in the local corpus**. For any specific offence: say it is not
> here and point to the full corpus (`./launch.sh`) or Fedlex. Do **not** quote an offence from memory.
