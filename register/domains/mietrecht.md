# Mietrecht — tenancy

Governing law: **OR (SR 220)** · file `cc-27-317_321_377.md` · Stand 2026-01-01 · coverage ✓ (Art. 253–273c present)
Scope: lease of residential and commercial property, OR Art. 253–273c. Cantonal law governs many *formalities*
(the Mietzins-Formular, Schlichtungsbehörde organisation) — flag it when a quoted article points to «Kanton».
