# Datenschutz — data protection

Governing law: **DSG (SR 235.1)** · file `cc-2022-491.md` · Stand 2025-07-07 · coverage ✓ (Art. 1–74, the 2020 nDSG)
Scope: the revised Datenschutzgesetz (in force since 1 Sept 2023). The implementing **DSV (SR 235.11)** sits in
the full corpus (via `catalog.md`); cantonal/public-body data protection is separate.
